About Mang Inasal

Mang Inasal, which means "Mr. Barbecue" in Ilonggo, was founded in 2003 in Iloilo City. It went to become one of the Philippines' most popular fast-food chains, known for its savory grilled chicken, unlimited rice offerings, and native-inspired dining experience.

What Makes Mang Inasal Different?

  • Signature Chicken Inasal grilled to perfection
  • Unlimited rice promotions loved by Filipinos
  • Affordable meals with authentic Filipino flavor
  • A relaxed and culturally themed environment
