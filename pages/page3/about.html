About Mang Inasal

Mang Inasal, which translates to “Mr. Barbecue” in Ilonggo, was founded in 2003 in Iloilo City. It quickly became one of the Philippines’ most successful fast-food chains by offering delicious grilled chicken, rice meals, and the iconic “unli-rice” promo that Filipinos love.

Why Mang Inasal Stands Out

  • Signature Chicken Inasal with native-style grilling
  • Affordable meals with unlimited rice
  • Filipino-themed dining experience
  • Widespread presence across the country
  • Also I love roasted chicken more than fried chicken in general
